# 招生信息 / Recruiting

SOARLAB（飞行通用智能课题组）现面向 2027 届相关专业同学，招收对具身智能、机器人及核心算法感兴趣的直博生、普博生和硕士研究生。课题组即将在南京大学智能科学与技术学院运行，由徐浩老师独立指导。

SOARLAB is recruiting direct Ph.D., regular Ph.D., and master's students from the 2027 cohort who are interested in embodied intelligence, robotics, and core algorithms. The lab will be led independently by Hao Xu at the School of Intelligent Science and Technology, Nanjing University.

## 研究主线与平台 / Research Lines and Platforms

我们围绕“两个半”硬件平台展开：无人机、人形机器人，以及正在推动的飞行人形机器人。

Our hardware platforms are aerial robots, humanoid robots, and the emerging direction of flying humanoid robots.

| 方向 / Direction       | 内容 / Scope                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Robotics Agent         | 面向 Agent 的感知、规划控制、世界模型与仿真等使能技术。 Agent-oriented perception, planning/control, world models, and simulation.                                                |
| WAM/VLA 机器人基础模型 | WAM/VLA 驱动的机器人基础模型，是当前最急需推进的方向之一。 WAM/VLA-driven robot foundation models are among our most urgent directions.                                           |
| 经典机器人核心算法     | 分布式感知、SLAM、无人机集群状态估计、强化学习与自主集群系统。 Distributed perception, SLAM, aerial swarm state estimation, reinforcement learning, and autonomous swarm systems. |

除了传统无人机和机器人系统的全栈积累，课题组已有正在迭代的 Agent 框架和正在训练的 WAM 框架，期待新同学加入共同建设。

Beyond full-stack aerial and robotic systems, the lab has an evolving agent framework and a WAM training pipeline under development. New students will help turn these into reliable research platforms.

## 培养理念与资源 / Mentoring and Resources

以高水平前沿探索和论文为核心，结合学生基础与兴趣确定课题，反对为发论文而灌水。课题主要围绕不低于 IROS/ICRA/CORL/RSS/RAL 水平的机器人研究进行投稿。

Research topics will be built around high-quality frontier exploration and publications, matched to each student's background and interests. We do not pursue low-quality paper production. The target bar is no lower than IROS, ICRA, CoRL, RSS, or RAL-level robotics work.

培养方式强调高自由度和高要求：每人方向相对独立，时间灵活、不打卡，但需保持合理沟通频率，不能人间消失。每周一次组会，两周一次一对一（线上或线下）。徐浩老师会亲自带学生上手，提供基础代码，或先做一个最小可跑 demo 帮助热启动；卡住时会一起 debug，必要时也会一起处理硬件问题。

The lab values freedom with responsibility. Students work on relatively independent directions with flexible schedules and no clock-in requirement, but must maintain regular communication. We run weekly group meetings and biweekly one-on-one meetings. Hao Xu will provide starter code or minimum runnable demos when useful, debug with students when they are blocked, and stay hands-on with hardware when necessary.

资源方面，课题组将尽力保障计算服务器、实验场地和无人机、机器人等实体验证平台。考虑到正在入职，前期免不了一起建设。对合适的同学，可推荐至业内头部合作单位实习。按照学院惯例发放科研补贴，不低于平均水平。

We will work to secure compute servers, experimental space, and physical validation platforms including UAVs and robots. Because the lab is being built during the transition to NJU, early members should expect to help build infrastructure. Strong students may be recommended for internships at top industrial partners. Research stipends will follow school practice and will not be below the average level.

## 对你的期待 / What We Expect

1. **思想品德 / Character.** 爱国敬业，拥护社会主义核心价值观；首先需要通过南京大学智能科学与技术学院的保研、考研或申博考核。 Applicants must meet the admission standards of the School of Intelligent Science and Technology, Nanjing University.

2. **学术基础 / Academic foundation.** 数理与编程功底扎实，对多模态大模型、强化学习、机器学习、SLAM 等方向有经验或强烈兴趣。欢迎数学、物理、人工智能、计算机等相关专业同学跨考。 Strong mathematics and programming foundations are expected. Experience or strong interest in multimodal models, RL, machine learning, or SLAM is welcome. Students from mathematics, physics, AI, computer science, and related fields are encouraged to apply.

3. **动手与实验精神 / Hands-on robotics.** 我们坚持“没有实物实验，就没有真正的机器人学和具身智能”。希望你愿意学习焊接、组装、3D 打印，能操控航模或机器人。有 RoboMaster 等机器人/无人机竞赛经验者优先。 We believe real robotics and embodied intelligence require physical experiments. Students should be willing to solder, assemble, 3D-print, operate aircraft models or robots, and work rigorously with real hardware. RoboMaster or UAV/robotics competition experience is a plus.

4. **AI 工具与自学能力 / AI-tool literacy and self-learning.** 入组后会安排自学计划，课题组统一购买 AI 工具 token。欢迎能让 AI 承担编程中 90% 以上工作量的同学，但前提是你有合格的编程基础，能纠正模型错误，并理解计算机组成原理、大模型基本架构和 RL 数学原理。 The lab will organize self-study plans and purchase AI-tool tokens. We welcome students who can make AI handle more than 90% of programming workload, provided they have enough programming and systems knowledge to detect and correct model errors.

5. **合作与可靠性 / Reliability.** 课题组风气自由，但学业压力不小。希望每位同学友善、团结、乐于合作，并在具身智能/机器人方向做出扎实成果。 The lab culture is open, but the academic pressure is real. We expect kindness, teamwork, reliability, and serious contributions to embodied intelligence and robotics.

## 项目、实习与毕业 / Projects, Internships, and Graduation

课题组承接的项目以有发表潜力的高水平工作为主，兼顾重要纵横向课题，合作方多为具身智能头部企业。项目投入不会超过总学习研究时间的一半，但需要时该顶就要顶，也不鼓励刻意推脱。鼓励高年级学生进入合作企业或经导师同意的业内头部单位实习，实习期间按企业标准发放工资。

Lab projects will mainly support high-level publishable research, while also covering important government and industry collaborations. Industrial partners are expected to include leading embodied-intelligence companies. Project work should not exceed half of total study and research time, but students are expected to contribute when needed. Senior students are encouraged to intern at partner companies or other top industry labs with advisor approval; internship salaries follow company standards.
