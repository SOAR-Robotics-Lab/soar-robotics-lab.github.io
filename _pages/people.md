    <p>Hao Xu received his Ph.D. from the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at HKUST, supervised by Prof. Shaojie Shen. He previously worked at DJI as a multi-sensor information fusion algorithm engineer and at Beihang University as an associate researcher. His research interests include embodied intelligence, robotics agents, robot foundation models, SLAM for aerial and legged robots, aerial swarm state estimation, distributed SLAM, and autonomous swarm systems.</p>
